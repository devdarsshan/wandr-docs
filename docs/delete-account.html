Delete Your WANDR Account

WANDR allows you to delete your account and all associated data directly from the app.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the WANDR app on your device
  2. Go to the Profile tab
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your identity (password or Google sign-in)
  5. Your account and data will be permanently deleted

What Data Is Deleted

  • Your profile information (name, photo, phone number, blood group)
  • Your emergency contact details
  • All trip history and location data
  • Chat messages you sent in groups and trips
  • Your group memberships
  • Plans you created
  • Profile pictures and uploaded media
  • Push notification tokens

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be removed immediately.

Timeframe

Most data is deleted immediately upon account deletion. Residual data in automated backups may take up to 30 days to be fully purged.

Need Help?

If you are unable to access the app or need assistance with account deletion, please contact us at support.wandr@gmail.com with the email address associated with your WANDR account.
